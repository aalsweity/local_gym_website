Welcome to the Power House Gym

How TOUGH do you wanna be?

You've come to the right place to get fit!

If you wanna get fit the right way, then you need to show commitment and start healthy habbits!

    Step 1

    Commit to a healthy eating style.
    If you think you're gonna lose weight or get fit by gyming and eating junk, then think again!
    Because eating junk and exercising only cancels each other out so that you gain absolutely nothing! Also, maybe some more weight.

    Step 2

    Commit to gym for real!
    Now that you got your junk food in the garbage, it's time to hit the gym everyday at least 1 hour a day!
    It doesn't matter if you can't run for an hour. Just doing some exercising for at least 1 hour is all you need to start a healthy gym life!

Step 3

Staying Commited till you reach your goal and beyond!
Once you have mastered the first 2 steps, step 3 should be a breeze!
Simply keep coming everyday and push it to the limit!
When you feel even more stronger and energetic, you may even decided to hit the gym twice a day instead of once. In the end, it depends on you!
So come on over and hit the gym with your gym partners and work together in achieving your goal!
